My Graphic Design Work

Orbit

This piece showcases a man and a woman in space as if in a dance with celestial bodies. "Orbit" was created using Procreate, drawn from my iPad. The creation process involved combining it with a poetic narrative (accredited to another student) that discusses the otherworldly feel, along with a blend of Adobe Photoshop and InDesign.

Remember?

"Remember?" is an evocative piece that delves into the themes of memory and identity. Utilizing Procreate, Photoshop, and InDesign, this artwork juxtaposes the image of an individual in a red outfit with fragmented text, creating a compelling interplay between image and word that echoes the confusion and loss associated with memory deterioration.
